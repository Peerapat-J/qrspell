macOS menu bar utility

Scan QR codes from your screen, quickly.

QRSpell is a small native macOS app for detecting QR codes from visible screen content, copying results, and keeping recent scans available on your Mac.

What It Does

  • Scans QR codes from selected areas or visible screens.
  • Processes scan results locally on your Mac.
  • Copies results to the clipboard when enabled.
  • Keeps scan history locally for quick access.

Privacy First

QRSpell does not use analytics, ads, tracking, telemetry, or server upload. Screen content is used only to detect QR codes on your device.
