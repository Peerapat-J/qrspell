Privacy

Your screen content stays on your Mac.

QRSpell does not include analytics, ads, tracking, telemetry, or server upload. Screen Recording permission is used only to detect QR codes from visible screen content.

FAQ

A few practical notes.

Does QRSpell scan camera input?

No. QRSpell focuses on QR codes already visible on your Mac screen.

Does scan data leave my Mac?

No. QR detection, history, clipboard handling, and notifications are local app behavior.

Why does it need Screen Recording permission?

macOS requires permission before an app can read visible screen content for QR detection.
