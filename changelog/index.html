Release notes for QRSpell.

1.0 - Release

Initial QRSpell release for Mac screen QR scanning.

  • Lightweight menu bar app for quick access.
  • Custom keyboard shortcut support.
  • Area and full-screen scan modes.
  • Multiple QR code support.
  • Popup results or clipboard-only result handling.
  • Recent scan history for looking back at earlier QR results.
  • Offline, local QR detection with no analytics or tracking.
  • One-time purchase with no subscription.
