QRSpell Help Center

FAQ

Can QRSpell stay quiet after scanning?

Yes. You can hide popups, sounds, and notification badges, then copy detected values to the clipboard or find them later in your scan history.

Can I look back at earlier scans?

Yes. QRSpell keeps recent scan results in your scan history.

Does QRSpell need the internet to scan?

No. QR detection runs locally on your Mac.

Are my scan results private?

Yes. Scan results never leave your Mac: no servers, tracking, data collection, analytics, or advertising.

Why does QRSpell need Screen Recording permission?

macOS requires Screen Recording permission before an app can read visible screen content for QR detection.

What if QRSpell needs Screen Recording permission but does not appear in System Settings?

If this happens, add QRSpell manually. Open System Settings, go to Privacy & Security, then Screen & System Audio Recording. Click the plus button at the bottom of the list, then add QRSpell from your Applications folder.

Is QRSpell a subscription?

No. QRSpell is planned as a one-time purchase with no subscription.
