FAQ

What does QRSpell scan?

QRSpell scans QR codes that are already visible on your Mac screen.

Can I use a keyboard shortcut?

Yes. QRSpell supports a custom shortcut so you can start scanning quickly.

Can I scan only part of the screen?

Yes. You can scan a selected area or use a full-screen scan mode when the code is already in view.

What happens when more than one QR code is found?

QRSpell supports multiple detected QR codes and lets you work with the available results.

Can QRSpell stay quiet after scanning?

Yes. You can show popup results or copy detected values to the clipboard.

Can I look back at earlier scans?

Yes. QRSpell keeps recent scan results available from history on your Mac.

Does QRSpell need the internet to scan?

No. QR detection runs locally on your Mac.

Why does QRSpell need Screen Recording permission?

macOS requires Screen Recording permission before an app can read visible screen content for QR detection.

Is QRSpell a subscription?

No. QRSpell is planned as a one-time purchase with no subscription.
